01 / The Eduba frame

A methodology that plugs into the programs you already run.

The idea is simple. About 60% of any program's operations is traditional database and code work. About 30% is rule-based logic. Around 10% is where real AI earns its keep. Eduba's work is helping teams sort their operations into those three layers before anything new is built, and publishing that methodology so partners can run it themselves.

Applied to a national-chapter nonprofit running Vets in AI, Fed Vets-to-Tech, an entrepreneurship pitch track, and a Summit: chapter event coordination is a database-and-scheduling problem. Cohort eligibility and partner-matching are rule-based. Curriculum refresh for AI content and outcome reporting to corporate sponsors are where an outside methodology partner lowers the internal load.

The shape we would propose is co-delivered cohort modules, Eduba-branded content inside Vets in AI, and shared authorship on methodology pieces for corporate sponsors.
